# 👤 User Guide

## Welcome

This guide will help you use the AI-Based Clothing Size Suggestion system to find your perfect fit.

## Getting Started

### 1. Browse Products

1. Navigate to the **Products** page
2. View available clothing items
3. Each product shows:
   - Product image
   - Product name
   - Two action buttons: "Buy Now" and "Try It"

### 2. Quick Purchase

If you know your size:

1. Click **"Buy Now"** on any product
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Shipping Address
   - Select your Size (S/M/L/XL/XXL)
3. Click **"Confirm Purchase"**
4. You'll see a success confirmation

### 3. AI Size Check (Recommended)

Not sure about your size? Let AI help!

1. Click **"Try It"** on any product
2. You'll be taken to the Size Checker page
3. Follow these steps:

## Using the Size Checker

### Step 1: Select a Product

1. Use the dropdown menu to select the product you're interested in
2. The product's measurements will be compared to yours

### Step 2: Upload Your Photo

**Photo Requirements for Best Results:**

✅ **DO:**
- Stand straight, facing the camera
- Keep arms slightly away from your body
- Ensure full body is visible (head to at least hips)
- Use good, even lighting
- Wear fitted clothing (not baggy)
- Use a plain background

❌ **DON'T:**
- Sit or lean
- Wear very loose/baggy clothes
- Have objects blocking your body
- Use poor lighting or shadows
- Crop out parts of your body

### Step 3: Analyze

1. Click the **"Analyze My Size"** button
2. Wait a few seconds for AI processing
3. View your results

### Understanding Results

The analysis shows:

#### Overall Fit Status
- 🟢 **Good Fit** - The product will fit you well
- 🟡 **Some adjustments needed** - May be slightly tight or loose
- 🔴 **May not fit well** - Consider a different size

#### Measurement Comparison

| Measurement | You | Product | Fit Status |
|-------------|-----|---------|------------|
| Shoulder | Your size | Product size | ✓ Normal / ⚠ Tight / ⚠ Loose |
| Chest | Your size | Product size | ✓ Normal / ⚠ Tight / ⚠ Loose |
| Sleeve | Your size | Product size | ✓ Normal / ⚠ Tight / ⚠ Loose |

#### Fit Descriptions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Normal** | Comfortable fit with standard room |
| **Tight** | May feel restrictive, consider sizing up |
| **Loose** | May feel baggy, consider sizing down |

### Step 4: Purchase

If you get a **Good Fit**:
1. You'll see a "This fits you perfectly!" message
2. Click **"Buy Now"** to purchase
3. Complete the purchase form

If the fit isn't ideal:
- Try a different product
- Check similar products in different sizes

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How accurate is the AI measurement?

The AI provides estimates based on visible body proportions. Accuracy depends on:
- Photo quality and lighting
- Pose and positioning
- Clothing visibility

**Tip:** For best results, follow all photo guidelines.

### Q: What if I get an error?

If you see "Could not detect body pose":
1. Make sure your full body is visible
2. Check lighting conditions
3. Remove any obstructions
4. Try a different photo

### Q: Can I try multiple products?

Yes! Use the product selector dropdown to compare yourself against different products without uploading a new photo.

### Q: Is my photo saved?

Photos are only used temporarily for analysis and are deleted immediately after processing. Your privacy is protected.

### Q: What measurements are checked?

| Measurement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Shoulder** | Width across shoulders |
| **Chest** | Chest/bust circumference |
| **Sleeve** | Arm length (shoulder to wrist) |
| **Neck** | Neck circumference (optional) |
| **Length** | Torso length (optional) |

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Use natural lighting** - Avoid harsh shadows
2. **Stand naturally** - Relaxed posture gives accurate results
3. **Wear form-fitting clothes** - For better landmark detection
4. **Try multiple photos** - If results seem off, try another photo
5. **Compare multiple products** - Find your best fit

## Need Help?

If you encounter issues:
1. Refresh the page and try again
2. Use a different browser
3. Contact support
